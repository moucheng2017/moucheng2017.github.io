Our <a href="https://conferences.miccai.org/2022/papers/066-Paper2505.html">Bayesian Pseudo Labels: Expectation Maximization for Robust and Efficient Semi-Supervised Segmentation</a> has been nominated and shortlisted for the MICCAI Young Scientist Award 2022! 

MICCAI Young Scientist Awards recognise the best papers at the MICCAI main conference, regarded as one of the most prestigious and competitive awards in the field of medical image computing. In 2022, only 15 out of 1825 submissions were shortlisted, making our paper a top 0.8% submission.
